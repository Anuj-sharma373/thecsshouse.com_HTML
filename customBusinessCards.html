Business cards are small sized cards which include details of your business. Business cards are also known as visiting cards. Business cards plays the key role in branding and act as a visual extension of your brand.

Business card fulfills many basic needs: advertisement, call to action and contact information. Business cards must be right designed because it is necessary for a visiting card to fit in the pocket. CSSHouse design the superior quality cards & right sized cards, which creates a great impression and make your business growing long lasting customers & passing to strangers.

What a right business card design contains?

1. Card shape and size

First and most important card must be pocket sized; card should be in such shape it should look genuine. Size of the card depends on the standards of the country, North American standard: 3.5 x 2 in., European standard: 3.346 X 2.165 in. Size of the card doesn’t matter much but you should keep three factors in mind: Bleed area, Trim line and Safety line.

2. Logo and Graphics

Now add your logo to the card and add some graphics to it, through which it will become attractive. Don’t forget that your card should have two sides then you can add logo, graphics to the one side and your business details on the other side. There must be no empty space fill it up with graphics.

3. Add text

  • Add name
  • Add company name.
  • Add job title
  • Add social media address
  • Add QR code
  • Add phone number
  • Add email
  • Add social media
  • Add you office address

4. Choose your typography

Once you know what you want in your card then you can choose how it will look. Typography is very important when it is about business cards, in this you have to make text legible and work in small space.
